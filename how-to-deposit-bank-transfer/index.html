There’s a fast and free way to deposit

You can top up your Skrill account for free with a bank transfer.

Sign up to Skrill

Simply make a bank transfer to Skrill to top up

  1. Create a Skrill account and select the bank transfer option when you deposit.
  2. Copy the bank details and the reference number.
  3. From your bank account, make a transfer to Skrill using these details.
  4. Don’t forget to add the reference number. It will help us process your deposit faster.
